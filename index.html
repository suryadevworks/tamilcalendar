About This Tamil Calendar / இந்த தமிழ் நாட்காட்டி பற்றி

This free online Tamil Calendar (தமிழ் நாட்காட்டி) provides accurate daily Tamil dates, Tamil months (Madam), and Panchangam data for Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. Solar positions are calculated using the Swiss Ephemeris with Lahiri ayanamsa — the same system used in official Tamil Nadu Government almanacs.

Sun transit dates (Rasi Peyarchi / ரவி பெயர்ச்சி) are determined using the traditional 13-Ghatika rule (1 Ghatika = 24 minutes; 13 Ghatikas = 312 minutes after sunrise). If the Sun enters a new Rasi within 312 minutes of sunrise, that very day becomes Tamil Month Day 1; otherwise the following day is Month Day 1.
